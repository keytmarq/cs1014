Game Mechanics

- Twisted Pathways -

In a quiet town there were twin sisters Isabelle and Lily, who is known for their inseperable bond. However, one day, Lily got seperated by an unusual creature. Isabelle is now trying to find her sister, to find her sister she has to go through a maze. Will she find Lily or will she get lost by herself in the twisted pathways?

She has to figure it on her own, without anyone, and she is lost in a mze where no one could find her. She shouts for help, but she has not other choice but to help herself and find for her twin sister.

- Whispers in the Walls -

In a quiet hometown in the woods there are a couple who are just recently got married, they went to the woods to have a honeymoon, not knowing there already living spirtis who are living there to make their stay miserable.
